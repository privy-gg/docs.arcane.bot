# Role Management

Arcane's Role Management plugin automatically assigns roles on member join and enables reaction-based self-assignment of roles.

## Setup

- [Auto Roles](./setup/auto-roles)
- [Reaction Roles](./setup/reaction-roles)

## Auto Roles

Auto roles are automatically assigned to members when they join your server. 

## Reaction Roles

Reaction roles allow your members to select predefined roles by using reactions.
